Privacy

  • The app stores basic account information such as username/email and password hash.
  • Raw passwords are not stored.
  • The app stores the user's tracked manhwa, progress, preferences, recommendation feedback, and learned recommendation weights.
  • The app may call external services like AniList and MangaDex to fetch manhwa metadata, chapter info, and cover images.
  • The app does not intentionally sell or share user data.
  • Since this is a personal/demo project, users should avoid entering sensitive personal information.
